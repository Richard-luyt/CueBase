# CueBase Product Features

Your knowledge, right on cue.

## Personal Knowledge Base

Upload your PDF documents and build a private, searchable library. CueBase processes your files into semantic chunks and stores them securely with encryption at rest.

## AI Chat with Your Data

Chat with an AI assistant that answers using only your uploaded knowledge. Choose strict mode for document-only answers, or search mode to supplement with the model's knowledge when needed.

## Strict & Search Modes

**Strict mode**: Answers exclusively from your documents. If nothing relevant is found in your knowledge base, it will tell you so instead of guessing.

**Search mode**: Prioritizes your knowledge base but can supplement with the AI's general knowledge when the context is insufficient. 

## Secure & Private

Your documents are encrypted. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. We use industry-standard practices to protect your data and privacy.
